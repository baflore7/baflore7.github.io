What my dogs mean to me?

The Generations of Dogs I have had:

Childhood Dogs

During my childhood into my teen years, I was not the most social or most popular at school but I knew I had the best of friends at home waiting for me.

When I was around 9 years old and never had a dog as small as my first Chihuahua that we adopted on Thanksgiving in 2008. She came with the name Linda but had the cutest apple head ever!

She was a year old at the time but seemed very scared until she became use to my mom and I. However, of course being a little kid who was an outcast at school was ethralled by the fact I had someone who actually wanted to hang out with me, so I ended up asking for another one a year later.

That is when my mom and I were at the 99 cent store and saw someone selling a litter of white Chihuahuas and they ended up having one girl pup. Therefore, as one little kid does; I started begging for her and of course at some point my mom gave in and we took her home to Linda.

Linda and Bootsie became like mother and daughter but at times fought like sisters but at the end of the day you could tell Linda really loved having a friend who was furry just like her.
